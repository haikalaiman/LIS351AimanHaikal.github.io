Ariana Grande, The Amazing Singer

Who is Ariana Grande?

Ariana Grande-Butera is Ariana Grande's full name and she was born on June 26, 1993. She is a wonderful American singer, songwriter, and also actress. She is originally from Boca Raton, Florida. Ariana Grande started her magnificent career when she was 15 and she began with broadway musical at the 2008 Broadway musical 13. Then, she became famous after she got promoted to be in Nickelodeon TV series, Victorious as Cat Valentine. The public did not really realize that she could sing so she had less singing scenes in the show. However, in 2011, she got signed with Republic Records when they discovered her Youtube cover videos. In 2013, she commenced her amazing journey as a singer with her 1950s nfluenced pop and R&B debut album,Yours Truly (2013). The album topped the US Billboard 200 chart, while its lead single, "The Way", charted in the top ten of the US Billboard Hot 100. She also has her own website that is currently promoting her new studio album "Positions": www.arianagrande.com

She invented herself as one of the most biggest pop star of her generation. Her skilled and polished vocals and unique writing styles in her work have landed her to have had four studio albums atop the Billboard 200 and captured the public's imagination with singles like "thank u, next" and "7 rings." For more information, please visit: www.billboard.com/music/ariana-grande
