Studio Albums (EP)

Besides releasing her LP, she also has several EPs or extended play or extra album that have a few tracks only.
Being a huge pop star, she believes that releasing EPs once in awhile would be a great idea especially in making
her fans happy! Her most well-known EP is a Christmas EP, called Christmas & Chill.

Christmas & Chill

Christmas & Chill is her second EP, however, it is the most popular EP of hers. It was released on December 18, 2015. It has
received tons of good reviews especially during Christmas time as the album incorporates "unique" sound of Christmas music.
The album is a pop Christmas album and it features six tracks. It was the beginning when the fans and the critiques realized that
Ariana Grande loves R&B sound. To listen to the full album, please visit: music.apple.com/us/album/christmas-chill-ep/
