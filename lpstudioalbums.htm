Studio Albums (LP)

Ariana Grande began her journey as a huge pop artist by releasing her first long played studio album, Yours Truly in 2013.
Years later, she came out with a few more angelic and vocal-shining records in her albums.

  • Yours Truly (2013)
  • My Everything (2014)
  • Dangerous Woman (2016)
  • Sweetener (2018)
  • Thank U, Next (2019)
  • Positions (2020)

Yours Truly (2013)

Yours Truly is her debut studio album and it was released on September 3, 2013 by her recording label, Republic Records.
The album is much influenced by pop and R&B features as you can see her explore different soundscapes in this wonderful album.
She said that this album reminded her of her diary and she wanted everyone to listen to every track as a story of her personal life.
This album features 13 songs. There are some hit songs on the album such as "The Way (feat. Mac Miller)" and "Almost is Never Enough."
To listen to the full album, please visit: music.apple.com/us/album/yours-truly/

My Everything (2014)

My Everything is her second studio album and it was released on August 25, 2014 by Republic Records. This album signifies
her growth from her past album and she wants to dive into a different soundscape like EDM&B hybrid. She worked with
many great artists and songwriters on this album as she wanted to produce one of the best albums in that period.
The album is a perfect definition of pop in 2014 as it soars with songs like "Break Free" and "Love Me Harder". This album features
15 songs. To listen to the full album, please visit: music.apple.com/us/album/my-everything-deluxe/

Dangerous Woman (2016)

Dangerous Woman is her third studio album and it was released on May 20, 2016 by Republic Records. It is definitely a bop. It has
received tons of good reviews from her fan "Arianators" and also critiques. People have acclaimed that it is the best album from her
as it showcases her ferocious voice with a newfound edge. Her most claimed tracks on this album are "Dangerous Woman" and "Side
to Side". To listen to the full album, please visit: music.apple.com/us/album/dangerous-woman/

Sweetener (2018)

Sweeteener is her fourth studio album and it was released on August 17, 2018 also by Republic Records. It was one of her most unexpected
albums after the tragic suicide bombing happened at one of her concerts. Many people did not like the album as she explored a new
soundscape called trap. However, it received rather mixed reviews from fans and critiques. Hit songs like "God is a Woman" and "Breathin"
became the favorites of all time. The album features 15 songs. To listen to the full album, please visit: music.apple.com/us/album/sweetener/

Thank U, Next (2019)

Thank U, Next is her fifth studio album and it was released on February 8, 2019 by Republic Records. This album is also unexpected because the
fans did not expect her to come up with a new album in such a short period of time. It was a surprise to Arianators and also the world. This album
is very personal to her after the death of Mac Miller, her ex beau and also her ruined engagament. The album essentially has received good reviews
and rated as one of her best albums of all times. Many songs were topped on Billboard such as "Thank U, Next," "7 rings" and etc. To listen to the
full album, please visit: music.apple.com/us/album/thank-u-next/

Positions (2020)

Positions is her sixth studio album and it was released on October 30, 2020. This is a surprise album to her fans. She tweeted and said "I cant wait
to give you my album this month" and the whole fandom went crazy. It is up to date her most matured album and very flirtatious. The album dives
into her current state of love and happiness with a hint of sexual elements. To listen to the full album, please visit: music.apple.com/us/album/positions/
